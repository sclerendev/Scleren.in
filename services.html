We build our own games and collaborate with others who do. Our role is to support game creators through strategic partnerships and integrate brand products directly into our games in ways that feel organic, immersive, and aligned with the game’s world.

Game Collaborations

Collaborations with creators and studios to integrate assets into live and upcoming game projects.

We collaborate with creators and studios who want their assets featured inside real game environments. SCLEREN does not produce 3D models internally. Instead, we work with external artists and developers to integrate their existing assets — especially vehicle models — into our game projects through structured collaboration. Car models, vehicles, and related assets are evaluated for quality, performance, and compatibility before integration.The goal is mutual visibility, clean integration, and ongoing collaboration between creators and the project. All integrations are handled with respect to the original creator’s ownership and intent.

Vehicle Collaboration Focus We actively collaborate with car and vehicle model creators interested in having their work featured inside gameplay environments. This includes road cars, concept vehicles, and custom builds.

In-Game Brand Integration

Brands are integrated as part of the game’s systems and world logic — never as forced overlays. Every placement is designed to respect immersion, player behavior, and long-term brand value.

Brand integrations are designed as part of the game system itself — not layered on after production. We embed brands only where they align with world logic, player behavior, and core mechanics, ensuring placements feel native rather than promotional.

Integrations range from environmental storytelling and product-authentic assets to UI-level exposure and mechanic-linked sponsorships. Each execution is scoped with clear visibility rules, performance constraints, and creative boundaries to protect immersion and player trust.

The result is brand presence that earns attention organically, survives long play sessions, and delivers value without compromising the integrity of the game world or the brand itself.
